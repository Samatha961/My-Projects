Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A classic Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 60ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the sliced garlic.
  3. Cook until the garlic is golden brown, then add the red pepper flakes.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan, tossing to coat in the oil and garlic.
  5. Season with salt and garnish with fresh parsley and Parmesan cheese if desired.
